About Us

PeriodHealth is an initiative by undergraduate students to spread awareness about menstruation. Such an issue becomes especially important in a country like India where menstruation is still an extremely taboo topic in most households. We aim to do this by providing job opportunities and distributing sanitary products.

MENSTRUAL HYGIENE

What is Menstrual Hygiene and why is it important?

Menstrual Hygiene is vital to the well-being of women and girls worldwide. Lack of access to basic hygiene products has led women to use unhygienic materials. Unhygienic practices during menstruation are associated with negative clinical outcomes including reproductive and urinary tract infections, anemia, and social isolation. Thus it is imperative to ensure menstrual hygiene and health.
